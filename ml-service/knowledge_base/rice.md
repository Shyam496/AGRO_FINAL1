# Crop Knowledge Base: Rice (Paddy)

## Overview
Rice is a staple crop in India, requiring standing water and high humidity.

## Soil Requirements
- Clayey or loamy soils are ideal as they retain water.
- pH range: 5.5 to 7.0.

## Irrigation
- Requires 120-150 cm of water throughout the growth cycle.
- "AWD" (Alternate Wetting and Drying) is a technique to save water.

## Fertilization
- Nitrogen (N) is crucial for vegetative growth.
- Common fertilizers: Urea, DAP, MOP.
- Recommended NPK: 120:60:60 kg/ha for high-yielding varieties.

## Common Diseases
- Rice Blast: Diamond-shaped lesions.
- Brown Spot: Small, circular brown lesions on leaves.
- Bacterial Leaf Blight: Water-soaked streaks starting from leaf tips.
