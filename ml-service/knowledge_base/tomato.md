# Crop Knowledge Base: Tomato

## Overview
Tomatoes are warm-season crops that grow best in temperate climates.

## Soil Requirements
- Well-drained sandy loam soil rich in organic matter.
- pH range: 6.0 to 7.0.

## Irrigation
- Requires consistent moisture but cannot tolerate waterlogging.
- Drip irrigation is highly recommended to prevent leaf diseases.

## Fertilization
- High Phosphorus (P) at planting for root development.
- Nitrogen (N) after first fruit set.
- Common fertilizers: NPK 19-19-19, Urea.

## Common Diseases
- Early Blight: Target-like concentric rings on leaves.
- Late Blight: Water-soaked spots that turn dark brown/black.
- Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus (TYLCV): Leaves curl upwards and turn yellow.
